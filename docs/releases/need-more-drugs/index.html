Need More Drugs

SIG003 · 2025-12-23 · SIGIL.ZERO

Dyson Hope

Hard, dark, funky breakbeat with Industrial-tinged techno, bass house, and progressive house remixes.

“Need More Drugs” is a dark, driving breakbeat cut that sits at the intersection of funky groove and hard bass-drivin grit. Dyson Hope’s remix pushes the tension into peak-time dark techno territory, while Brian G's rework twists the original into a deep, progressive house mutation. The flip, Disco Fever, gets an original treatment from Dyson Hope and NOTOTO showcasing hard bassy breakbeat while Dyson Hope's remix reinterprets it as a bass house jam.
